# Morning Market Checklist

## Pre-Analysis (6:45 - 7:00 AM MT)
- [ ] Wake up, water, coffee
- [ ] Quick glance at overnight futures (ES, NQ)
- [ ] Any major overnight news?
- [ ] Clear head — no trading decisions yet

## Analysis Review (7:00 - 7:30 AM MT)
- [ ] Read teacher's market analysis
- [ ] Review @satymahajan's day trade idea (Alma auto-sends at 7:15 AM)
- [ ] Note the bias (bullish/bearish/neutral)
- [ ] Key levels to watch (support/resistance)
- [ ] Any specific setups mentioned?
- [ ] Set price alerts for key levels

## Market Open (7:30 - 7:45 AM MT)
- [ ] Watch, don't trade the first 5-15 minutes
- [ ] Note opening direction vs. expected bias
- [ ] Is volume confirming the move?
- [ ] Patience — let the setup come to you

## Trading Window (7:45 AM+ MT)
- [ ] Only take planned setups
- [ ] Position size correct?
- [ ] Stop loss set BEFORE entry
- [ ] Know your exit (profit target)

---

## Notes
- Wild opens = wait longer before acting
- If you missed the move, don't chase
- There's always another trade

*Update this as you learn what works for you.*
